Access

Eliminate cost and geography as barriers to digital access.

Community

Foster and support self-sustaining, community networks in both rural and urban areas.

Infrastructure

Acquire, make available, and support community-owned and managed infrastructure such as fiber rings and low-cost internet exchanges.

Knowledge

Perform and make available research on network hardware, software, and firmware as well as best management practices to advance community networks.

People

Empower all people to determine their digital future by investing in accessible technical training and resources.
